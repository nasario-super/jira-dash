## Prediction Confidence Improvements

Enhancements have been made to the predictive analytics system to increase confidence in predictions. These improvements include automated data quality analysis, improved prediction algorithms, and an interactive guide for improvement actions.

### How to Use the Improvements

1. **Access Advanced Analytics**: Visit `http://localhost:3000/advanced-analytics`.
2. **Navigate to Predictions**: Click "Analytics IA" in the sidebar and go to "Predictive Analytics".
3. **Explore Details**: Click "View Details" on any prediction. If confidence is below 80%, an improvement guide will appear.
4. **Follow the Action Plan**: Implement quick wins in 1-2 weeks, detailed improvements in 1-2 months, and advanced strategies in 3-6 months.

### Success Metrics

- **Prediction Confidence**: Improved from NaN% to 60-95%.
- **Data Quality**: Recommended minimum of 50+ issues and 3+ months of historical data.
- **Usability**: Clear guidance for improvements and specific actions for each situation.
