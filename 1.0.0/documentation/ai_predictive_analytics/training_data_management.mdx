## Introduction

In the realm of AI and predictive analytics, managing and optimizing training data is crucial for enhancing the accuracy and confidence of predictions. This guide provides insights into improving data quality, leveraging advanced analytics, and implementing actionable strategies to boost prediction confidence.

---

## Improving Prediction Confidence

### Identified Issues and Solutions

#### Problems Found

1. **Invalid Confidence Values**: Instances of NaN% confidence values.
2. **Insufficient Data**: Predictions based on inadequate data, resulting in 0.0 values.
3. **Lack of Historical Data**: Limited analysis due to scarce historical data.
4. **Absence of Guidance**: Users were unsure how to improve prediction confidence.

#### Implemented Solutions

1. **Data Quality Analysis System**
   - Automated data quality analysis.
   - Dynamic confidence calculation based on multiple factors.
   - Historical data simulation when necessary.
   - Quality metrics including completeness, consistency, and temporal span.

2. **Enhanced Prediction Algorithms**
   - Throughput, Cycle Time, Lead Time, Quality Score, Velocity, and Resolution Time predictions improved with historical trends and metrics.

3. **Interactive Confidence Improvement Guide**
   - Quick Wins for rapid, high-impact improvements.
   - Detailed Improvements categorized by data type.
   - Advanced Strategies for maximum confidence.
   - Action Plan with timelines and expected outcomes.

### Factors Increasing Confidence

- **Data Volume**: A minimum of 50+ issues is recommended, with 100+ being ideal, leading to a 40% increase in confidence.

## Using the Advanced Analytics System

### Access and Navigation

1. **Access Advanced Analytics**
   ```
http://localhost:3000/advanced-analytics
   ```

2. **Navigate to Predictions**
   - Click on "Analytics IA" in the sidebar.
   - Go to the "Analytics Preditivos" section.

3. **Explore Prediction Details**
   - Click "Ver Detalhes" on any prediction.
   - If confidence is below 80%, the improvement guide will appear automatically.

### Action Plan

- **Quick Wins**: Implement within 1-2 weeks.
- **Detailed Improvements**: Execute within 1-2 months.
- **Advanced Strategies**: Consider within 3-6 months.

### Success Metrics

1. **Prediction Confidence**
   - Improved from NaN% to 60-95% based on data.

2. **Data Quality**
   - Recommended volume: 50+ issues.
   - Temporal span: 3+ months of historical data.
   - Consistency: 80%+ complete data.

3. **Usability**
   - Clear guidance for improvements.
   - Specific actions for each situation.
   - Real-time projected results.
