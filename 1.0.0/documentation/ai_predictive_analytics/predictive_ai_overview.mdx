## Introduction

This documentation provides an overview of the predictive AI capabilities within the Advanced Analytics system. It highlights improvements made to enhance prediction confidence, data quality, and usability through intuitive interfaces and personalized actions.

## Prediction Confidence Improvements

### Identified Issues and Solutions

#### Problems Encountered

- **Invalid Confidence Values:** Instances of NaN% confidence.
- **Insufficient Data:** Predictions based on inadequate data.
- **Lack of Historical Data:** Limited analysis due to scarce historical data.
- **Absence of Guidance:** Users were unsure how to improve predictions.

#### Implemented Solutions

1. **Data Quality Analysis System**
   - Automated data quality analysis.
   - Dynamic confidence calculation based on multiple factors.
   - Simulation of historical data when necessary.
   - Quality metrics including completeness, consistency, and temporal span.

2. **Enhanced Prediction Algorithms**
   - Throughput, Cycle Time, Lead Time, Quality Score, Velocity, and Resolution Time predictions improved with historical trends and temporal analysis.

3. **Interactive Confidence Improvement Guide**
   - Quick Wins for rapid, high-impact improvements.
   - Detailed improvements categorized by data type.
   - Advanced strategies for maximum confidence.
   - Action plans with timelines and expected outcomes.

### Factors Increasing Confidence

- **Data Volume:** A minimum of 50 issues is recommended, with 100+ being ideal for a 40% confidence increase.

## How to Use the Improvements

1. **Access Advanced Analytics**
   - Navigate to [Advanced Analytics](http://localhost:3000/advanced-analytics).

2. **Navigate to Predictions**
   - Click "Analytics IA" in the sidebar and go to "Analytics Preditivos."

3. **Explore the Details**
   - Click "Ver Detalhes" on any prediction. If confidence is below 80%, the improvement guide will appear automatically.

4. **Follow the Action Plan**
   - Implement Quick Wins in 1-2 weeks.
   - Execute Detailed Improvements in 1-2 months.
   - Consider Advanced Strategies in 3-6 months.

### Updated Components

- **PredictionDetailsModal.tsx:** Offers confidence analysis, factors considered, and technical interpretation of results.

- **Technical Information:** Separate sections for technical details with actionable recommendations.

The Advanced Analytics system has been enhanced to provide more reliable and actionable predictions.
