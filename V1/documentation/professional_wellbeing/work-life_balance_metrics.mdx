# Work-Life Balance Metrics

## Introduction

In the realm of professional wellbeing, maintaining a healthy work-life balance is crucial for team productivity and individual satisfaction. This guide provides insights into tracking and improving work-life balance indicators using various metrics and dashboards. The focus is on leveraging data to monitor productivity, identify overload, and ensure quality in work processes.

## Key Features

### Productivity Metrics

- **Status Tracking**: Monitor the completion status of tasks to understand what has been delivered in the last week.
- **Workload Identification**: Identify potential overload by tracking the workload assigned to each team member.

### Agile Dashboard

The Agile Dashboard offers a comprehensive view of ongoing projects and tasks:

- **Total Issues**: Track the total number of issues in the current sprint.
- **Completion Percentage**: Monitor the percentage of issues completed.
- **Progress Tracking**: Keep an eye on issues in progress, blocked, or overdue.
- **Daily Scrum**: Utilize daily lists of issues to facilitate standups, identify blockers, and track sprint progress.

### Executive Dashboard

Designed for directors and managers, the Executive Dashboard provides key performance indicators (KPIs) to assess team efficiency:

- **Velocity**: Measures the number of issues completed per sprint.
- **Throughput**: Tracks the delivery rate.
- **Cycle Time**: Calculates the average time taken to resolve an issue.
- **Lead Time**: Measures the time from request to completion.
- **Efficiency**: Evaluates the percentage of issues completed.

### Quality Metrics

Quality metrics are essential for maintaining high standards in project delivery:

- **Defect Rate**: Compares bugs found against total issues, with ideal rates being less than 20%.
- **Bug Resolution Time**: Categorizes resolution speed into rapid (&lt;2 days), normal (2-5 days), and slow (&gt;5 days).
- **Test Coverage**: Monitors the percentage of issues with tests and bugs found post-release.
- **Rework**: Tracks the ratio of reopened issues to resolved issues, with a low rework rate being favorable.

## Analytics and Insights

### Automated Insights

Leverage AI-driven analytics to generate automatic insights, including:

- **Trends**: Identify patterns in velocity and activity peaks.
- **Anomaly Detection**: Spot sudden drops in velocity or unusual increases in bugs.
- **Forecasting**: Predict sprint completion timelines and issue counts for the upcoming month.
- **Recommendations**: Receive actionable advice such as prioritizing critical bugs or redistributing workload.

### Data Aggregation and Correlation

- **Data Aggregation**: Aggregate data by status, project, or user to provide comprehensive insights.
- **Correlation Analysis**: Examine the relationship between metrics, such as velocity versus bug rates, to identify potential quality issues.

## Summary

This guide outlines the tools and metrics available for monitoring work-life balance and productivity within teams. By utilizing dashboards and analytics, organizations can gain valuable insights into team performance, identify areas of overload, and maintain high-quality standards in project delivery. The focus on data-driven decision-making ensures that work-life balance is not only monitored but actively improved.

For more detailed instructions and access to the dashboards, please refer to the specific documentation and guides provided in the system.
